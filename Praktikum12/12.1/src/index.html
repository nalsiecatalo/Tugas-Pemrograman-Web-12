GET TO KNOW ENHYPEN!

Who is Enhypen?
ENHYPEN (엔하이픈) is the final 7 members of the survival show I-LAND under BE:LIFT Lab, a joint label created by HYBE and CJ E&M Entertainment (previously known as BigHit Entertainment).
How about the members?
The group consists of Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki.
Enhypen meanings
ENHYPEN is a mix of an en dash and a hyphen that connects different words to create new meaning, members of ENHYPEN will connect, discover each other, and grow together.
Fandom's name
ENGENE is the official fandom name for the South Korean boy group ENHYPEN. The name was announced on October 9th, 2020.
